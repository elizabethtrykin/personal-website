Hi, I'm Elizabeth

In the past few years I've spent a lot of time thinking about why people still die from diseases, genetic conditions, doctor's mistakes and why there is so little being done about this. At first, my go-to solution was to become a surgeon, which would mean I would have a little bit of control over this... but I think I've found a better solution.

I want to go beyond impacting only a small fraction of the world, as well as live a meaningful, impactful, exciting and challenging life. This is only the beginning of my journey to 10x myself and become the best version of myself.

If you'd like to receive updates on what I'm up to and support me on my journey, I send out a monthly newsletter.
